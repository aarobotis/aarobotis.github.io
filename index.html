Alekos Robotis

I am a fifth year graduate student at Cornell University working under the supervision of Daniel Halpern-Leistner. I am interested in derived and triangulated categories and moduli spaces in algebraic geometry. This semester (spring 2024) I am a Stephen Della Pietra Fellow at MSRI/SLMath in Berkeley, California affiliated with the research semester in noncommutative algebraic geometry.

Together with Jeffrey Jiang, I co-founded the Cornell DRP in Spring of 2020. I am currently a co-organizer of the DRP, together with Chase Vogeli and Sara Venkatraman.

I also sometimes answer questions on math stack exchange.
