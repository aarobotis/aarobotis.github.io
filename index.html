Alekos Robotis

I am interested in derived and triangulated categories and moduli spaces in algebraic geometry, with a particular emphasis on Bridgeland stability conditions. I recently completed my PhD at Cornell University working under the supervision of Daniel Halpern-Leistner. This fall, I will be an NSF postdoctoral fellow in the department of mathematics of Columbia University, where I am co-organizing the postdoc algebraic geometry seminar
