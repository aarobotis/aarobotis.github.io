Alekos Robotis

I am an NSF postdoctoral fellow at Columbia University interested in derived and triangulated categories and moduli spaces in algebraic geometry, with a particular emphasis on Bridgeland stability conditions. I recently completed my PhD at Cornell University working under the supervision of Daniel Halpern-Leistner. This fall, I am co-organizing the postdoc algebraic geometry seminar

My CV is linked here. You can get in contact with me by email at a.mylastname@columbia.edu.
