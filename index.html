Alekos Robotis

I am a sixth year graduate student at Cornell University working under the supervision of Daniel Halpern-Leistner. I am interested in derived and triangulated categories and moduli spaces in algebraic geometry. In spring 2024 I was a Stephen Della Pietra Fellow at MSRI/SLMath in Berkeley, California affiliated with the research semester in noncommutative algebraic geometry. This semester (fall 2024) I will be on the job market for postdoctoral positions. You can find a copy of my CV here.

Together with Jeffrey Jiang, I co-founded the Cornell DRP in Spring of 2020. I am currently a co-organizer of the DRP, together with Chase Vogeli and Sara Venkatraman.

I also sometimes answer questions on math stack exchange.
