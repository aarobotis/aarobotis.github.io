Alekos Robotis

This fall, I will be an NSF postdoctoral fellow in the department of mathematics of Columbia University. I recently completed my PhD at Cornell University working under the supervision of Daniel Halpern-Leistner. I am interested in derived and triangulated categories and moduli spaces in algebraic geometry, with a particular emphasis on Bridgeland stability conditions. In the spring of 2024 I was a Stephen Della Pietra Fellow at MSRI/SLMath in Berkeley, California affiliated with the research semester in noncommutative algebraic geometry.

My CV is linked here. You can get in contact with me by email at ar2377"at"cor.....edu.
